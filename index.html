Gotipua


Gotipua is a traditional dance form from Odisha, India, where young boys dress as girls and perform graceful dances. The performance is known for its acrobatic moves and devotional themes, often linked to the worship of Lord Jagannath. Gotipua is considered the precursor to Odissi dance and is known for its vibrant costumes, intricate footwork, and expressive storytelling.
